About TPAR

What is it? Businesses in the building & construction industry must report payments made to subcontractors each financial year.

Who needs to lodge? Any business that pays subcontractors for building/construction services.

When? Due 28 August following the end of the financial year.

What you need: Each subcontractor's name, ABN, and total amount paid (including GST).

How to lodge: Export the CSV and provide it to your accountant, or lodge directly via ATO Online Services.

Tip: Make sure you record the ABN for each subcontractor in the Supplier Contact Book for accurate reporting.
