How Proprietor's Earnings is Calculated

Proprietor's Earnings = Revenue (ex GST) - Materials (at cost) - Labour (wages + super + PAYG) - Expenses

  • Revenue (ex GST): Payments received ÷ 1.1 (GST goes to ATO, not you)
  • Materials: Your actual cost, not markup price. The markup profit stays in your draw.
  • Labour: Worker wages + super (11.5%) + PAYG withheld. Owner labour is NOT deducted.
  • Expenses: General business expenses not linked to specific jobs

Data is recorded automatically at the start of each month. Use the backfill task to populate historical data.
