About These Deadlines

BAS — Quarterly GST reporting. Lodge via myGov or your BAS agent.

Super Guarantee — 11.5% of ordinary time earnings, paid quarterly to workers' super funds.

TPAR — Annual report of payments to subcontractors. Required for building & construction.

Insurance — Workers Comp and Public Liability typically renew annually. Dates shown are estimates.
