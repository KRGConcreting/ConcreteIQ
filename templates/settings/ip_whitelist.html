How It Works

Understanding IP whitelist behaviour

  • Empty whitelist = all IP addresses are allowed (default).
  • Localhost (127.0.0.1 and ::1) is always allowed, even when whitelist is active.
  • Public routes (customer portal, booking page) are never blocked by the whitelist.
  • Blocked requests are logged in the Security Audit Log.
