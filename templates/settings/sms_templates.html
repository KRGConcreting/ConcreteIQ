SMS Templates

Customise the SMS messages sent to customers. Use variables like {first_name} to personalise.

Available Variables

Use these placeholders in your templates — not all variables apply to every template

{first_name} {customer_name} {address} {business_name} {quote_number} {invoice_number} {total} {portal_url} {job_date} {eta} {days_overdue} {variation_amount}

Quotes & Jobs

Quote Sent

Sent when a new quote is sent to a customer

Manual

Variables: {first_name} {quote_number} {total} {business_name} {portal_url}

Quote Follow-up

Sent as a follow-up if the customer hasn't responded to a quote

Auto

Variables: {first_name} {quote_number} {business_name} {portal_url}

Variation Sent

Sent when a variation/amendment is sent for review

Manual

Variables: {first_name} {quote_number} {variation_amount} {business_name} {portal_url}

Scheduling

Auto

Job Reminder

Auto

Variables: {first_name} {address} {job_date} {business_name} {quote_number}

On My Way (no ETA)

SMS Character Limits

Standard SMS: 160 characters (1 segment). Longer messages are split into 153-character segments. Variables like {first_name} are replaced with actual values before sending.
